Our History

60+ Years of Engineering Experience

TechTeam Engineering's roots trace back more than 60 years of combined industrial engineering practice. Across generations, our teams have designed, built, and commissioned production lines and structural systems for manufacturers across food and beverage, building materials, and structural steel industries.

That history means that when a client comes to us with a challenge — a new production line, a structural system, a facility expansion — we've likely encountered a version of it before, and we bring that pattern-recognition to every new feasibility study and design.

Cost Control

Subcontractor Sourcing & Management

Part of delivering a competitive turn-key project is knowing where to manufacture. TechTeam identifies, vets, and manages a network of subcontractors across our regions of operation, allowing us to reduce manufacturing costs without compromising on quality or schedule.

Our engineering team stays involved through fabrication and assembly at every subcontracted facility, applying the same inspection standards we use in-house, so the client experiences a single, consistent quality bar from concept to commissioning.
