JK Structure Production Lines

TechTeam Engineering designs and delivers complete JK Structure production systems for JK Structure.

Engineering Scope

Integrated production lines for JK structure manufacturing

Our JK Structure systems are engineered for high-throughput production with precise control over panel geometry, core density, skin alignment, and final handling. Each line is tailored to the customer’s product mix, panel thickness, and target output.

The line concept typically includes material handling, forming stations, core application, curing and cooling sections, panel cutting, and automated stacking and transfer systems. We support both PU and Rockwool solutions with the appropriate process design and equipment integration.
