What We Design

From beverage production to structural panel systems, TechTeam engineers the machinery and structures that keep industrial facilities running.

01 · Structural Systems

3D Steel Structure Mesh Panel Systems

Welded 3D steel mesh panel systems combine steel wire mesh with a rigid insulation core, engineered for use as structural walls and slabs in residential, commercial, and industrial construction.

TechTeam engineers both the panel production system and structural design guidance, supporting fast on-site assembly, thermal performance, and seismic resilience.

Beer Factory Production Systems

03 · Building Envelope

Sandwich Panel Production Lines

Continuous production lines for insulated sandwich panels, engineered for both PU (polyurethane) and Rockwool core materials.

Lines are designed for consistent core density, precise metal skin alignment, and dimensional accuracy across long production runs, supporting panel profiles used in roofing, walling, and cold storage construction.

04 · Metal Coating

Colour Coating Lines

TechTeam designs Un-Coiler to Re-Coiler colour coating lines for pre-painted metal coil, integrating chemical treatment, primer and top-coat application, and infrared curing ovens for fast, uniform curing.

Line layout and oven zoning are engineered around coil width, coating thickness, and target line speed, balancing throughput with coating quality.

05 · Panel Components

Stiffener Pillow Production Systems

Stiffener pillows reinforce panel systems at load-bearing points. TechTeam engineers automated production systems for manufacturing stiffener pillows at scale, with consistent geometry and material properties from batch to batch.

These systems are typically integrated alongside our sandwich panel and 3D mesh panel production lines to deliver a complete panel manufacturing capability.
