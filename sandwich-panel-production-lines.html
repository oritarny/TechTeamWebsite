Sandwich Panel Production Lines

TechTeam Engineering designs and delivers complete sandwich panel production systems for PU and Rockwool applications, from panel forming and core injection to cutting, stacking, and automated handling.

Engineering Scope

Integrated production lines for insulated panel manufacturing

Our sandwich panel systems are engineered for high-throughput production with precise control over panel geometry, core density, skin alignment, and final handling. Each line is tailored to the customer’s product mix, panel thickness, and target output.

The line concept typically includes material handling, forming stations, core application, curing and cooling sections, panel cutting, and automated stacking and transfer systems. We support both PU and Rockwool solutions with the appropriate process design and equipment integration.
